# Growth Momentum DCA Strategy

## Executive Summary

Growth Momentum DCA is a systematic dollar-cost averaging strategy designed to beat the S&P 500 by focusing on high-growth technology stocks with momentum confirmation.

## Signal Logic

- **Momentum Filter**: Only buys stocks above 20-day SMA
- **Market Filter**: Pauses when SPY RSI > 75 or VIX > 25
- **Execution**: 3-5 times daily during market hours
